Accomplishments

Mohamed Salah has left an effect on his teammates wherever he went, for the better, but the amount of awards he's received while on these teams is immeasurable. The rewards Salah has received during his career are as follows:

  • English Champion
  • UEFA Supercup Winner
  • 2X English League Cup winner
  • English FA Cup Winner
  • English Super Cup Winner
  • 11th in 2022-23 Ballon D'or Season
  • 2x Footballer of the Year
  • FIFA Puskas Award
  • TM-Player Of the Season
  • 2x Footballer of the Year
  • 2x English League Cup Winner
  • 2x Swiss Champion
  • 3x Golden Boot Award
  • PFA Player of the Year Award
