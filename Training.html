Training

Mohamed Salah's training can be split into three parts. The first is gym training, the second is soccer training with the club, and the third is training he does by himself which helps his overall body shape. Firstly, in the gym, Salah does weight training, core workouts, cardio, strength training, and recovery. With the club, they practice passing, shooting, and strategizing any new ways of positioning each other or scoring. When Salah isn't in the gym or with the club, he does yoga, calisthenics, and swimming. His training has many various exercises and without his discipline, he would not be able to do them every week nor reach where he is now.
